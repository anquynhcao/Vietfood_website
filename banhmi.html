Banh mi

Difficulty rating:

Easy

Introduction

Bánh mì, a delectable fusion of French and Vietnamese culinary influences, is a beloved street food staple that has captured the hearts and taste buds of food enthusiasts around the globe. Originating from the vibrant streets of Saigon, this iconic sandwich is a testament to Vietnam's rich cultural tapestry and its ability to adapt and innovate.

The ingredients are for 2-people portion.

Ingredients

Ingredients Unit Amount
French bread bread 2
pork meat gram 500
Galangal gram 30
lemongrass stack 2
tumeric gram 5
sugar/honey gram 50
salt gram 25
lime juice gram 10
soy sauce ml 40
sesame seeds - -
cucumber - 1
lettuce - 1
other veggies (optional) - -
bamboo skewers - a few
tomatoes - 2
chili peppers - 2
purple onion / shallots - 2
onion - 1
fish sauce, sugar tbsp to taste (1-2)

Instructions

  • Utensils: Charcoal grill, electric grill (if no charcoal grill is available), large bowl, tongs…
  • Clean pork thoroughly with salt water, then slice into thin, even pieces.
  • Clean and crush turmeric, galangal, and lemongrass for marinating the meat.
  • Marinate the sliced pork with the crushed galangal, lemongrass, turmeric, along with 25g of salt, 50g of white sugar (or honey), 10g of lime juice, 40ml of soy sauce, and a sprinkle of toasted sesame seeds. Wrap the marinated meat tightly and let it rest for at least 1 hour, allowing the flavors to meld.
  • Skewer marinated pork. Grill the skewered pork over charcoal for about 8-10 minutes, flipping regularly until cooked through and golden brown.
  • Sauce preparation:
  • Dice 3 tomatoes and 2 chili peppers. Finely chop purple onions and 1 onion. Sauté the purple onions until fragrant, then add the tomatoes and cook until soft. Season with 2 teaspoons of fish sauce and 1 teaspoon of sugar, stir well. Add the chopped chili peppers and onions to the mixture, bring to a boil, and adjust seasoning to taste. Pour the sauce into a bowl.
  • Slice bread and fill it with lettuce, herbs, and cucumber. Add the grilled pork and sauce to the sandwich. Serve and enjoy your delicious grilled pork sandwich!
