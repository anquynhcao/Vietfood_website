Pho Bo

Difficulty rating:

Medium

Introduction

Phở bò, a quintessential dish of Vietnamese cuisine, is a hearty and aromatic beef noodle soup that embodies the essence of Vietnamese flavors. Steeped in history and tradition, this beloved dish consists of tender slices of beef, rice noodles, and a fragrant broth infused with spices such as star anise, cinnamon, and cloves.

The ingredients are for 2-people portion.

Ingredients

Ingredients Unit Amount
Pho noodle (raw) gram 500
Beef sirloin gram 500
Onion/shallots - 2
Green Onion - 2
Coriander gram 100
Ginger gram 50
Beef bone gram 700
Dried cinnamon, cardamom, tangerine peel, star anise, cloves, coriander seeds pieces 2 each
Sugar tbsp 4
Salt tbsp 2
Water liter 3
Fish sauce tbsp 2
